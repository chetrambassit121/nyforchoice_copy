What is a Petition?

"A petition is a request to an authority, most commonly a government official or public entity." .....

What is a Online Petition?

"An online petition (or Internet petition, or e-petition) is a form of petition which is signed online, usually through a form on a website. Typically, after there are enough signatories, the resulting letter may be delivered to the subject of the petition, usually via e-mail." .....

Brief History of Petitions

"Petitioning was open to everyone, including people not eligible to vote, so it became an important means for expressing opinions, persuading legislators, and, ultimately, influencing the political landscape of the new nation. In fact, scholars have determined that petitioning led to more legislative action in early America than any other source." .....
