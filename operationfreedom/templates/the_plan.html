Online Signature Requirements

Using GoPetition to collect and display the online signatures.


The following information is required for the Petitions to be as official and efficient as possible!


  • Full Name: This is required to identify the individual signing. Can be displayed as Anonymous
  • Email: This is required to contact the individual signing with important updates and information.
  • Age: This is required to identify if the individual signing is an adult or under the age of 18. Both are valid to sign. Adults signing may be more official and a more powerful statement.
  • City or Town: This is required to ensure the individual signing is within New York City or the State of New York.
  • State: This is required to ensure the individual signing is within the State of New York
  • Country: This is required to ensure the individual signing is within the United States of America

All other information fields are optional but RECCOMENDED to be filled out. Comments are important to express your opinion as a New Yorker! It will deliver a stronger message to our Mayor and Governor.
